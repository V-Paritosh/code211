What is Code211?

Code211, a 10-hour coding event at Palatine High School, uniting students across the district. Explore workshops, compete in teams for prizes, and showcase your projects for a chance to win. Join us for a day of coding creativity and collaboration!

FAQ's

See some of the most asked questions about our event

Who can participate?

Anyone as long as you go to school in District 211!

Do I need to bring food?

Lunch will be provided from the school cafeteria and Dinner will be from an outside vendor - more detail coming soon

What should I bring?

A laptop, charger, and any other amenities you need. If you don't have a laptop, we will have a limited number onsite.

Will there be prizes?

Yes! Stay tuned for the prize distribution and categories.

What can I create?

Anything as long as it is school appropriate. You have full liberty to make games, apps, or websites. If you are struggling to think of ideas, visit chicagohacks.org to see winning projects and to explore another great hackathon.

What type of workshops will be there?

We will have workshops ranging from learning a language to Artificial Intelligence.
